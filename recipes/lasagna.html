Lasagna

How to make lasagna

  • Cheese
  • Ricotta
  • Egg
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato Paste
  • Noodles

Steps:

  1. Mix the ricotta and cheeses with an egg in a bowl, and set aside.

  2. Put the meat in a pot with onion, garlic and tomato paste

  3. Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with noodles and bake until bubbling
