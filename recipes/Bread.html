How to make bread

  • Warm Water
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Active Dry Yeast
  • Salt
  • Vegetable or Canola oil
  • Flour

Steps:

  1. Assemble bread ingeredients

  2. Dissolve the yeast and activate by proofing

  3. Add remaining indgredients and mix then knead the bread

  4. First rise, punch and shape it, second rise and bake the bread
